Manufacturers

Manufacturers produce products that are avilable on our exchange.

Products

Products are unique "templates" that are produced by manufacturers in batches.

Batches

Batches are collections of manufactured products that can be shipped.

Shipments

Shipments are (potentially) collections of batches that are scheduled to be shipped at a certain point in time. They contain broker's credentials and other useful information.
